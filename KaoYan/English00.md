| /ɑ:/  | 阿 (as in American "father")          | /ɒ/   | 喔(shorter, as in British "lot")           |
| /ɔ:/  | 噢  (like "aw" in "law")          | /ʊ/   | 乌(shorter, like "oo" in "book")           |

| /ɜ:/  | 儿(as in "bird")           | /ə/   | 呃 (schwa sound, like "a" in "about")           |
| /eɪ/  | 欸           | /aɪ/  | 爱 (diphthong in "high")           |
